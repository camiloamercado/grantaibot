## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can the AI access the internet or external databases?**
A: No. It only uses the documents you upload.

**Q: Are my uploaded documents saved permanently?**
A: No. Documents are held in browser memory only and are lost when you refresh or close the page. Re-upload each session.

**Q: Can I upload multiple files at once?**
A: Yes. Select multiple files in the upload dialog, or upload in batches.

**Q: The AI gave a wrong answer â€” what do I do?**
A: This can happen if the uploaded document doesn't cover the topic clearly. Always verify critical decisions with your Grants Manager. Report persistent issues to your IT team.

**Q: Can different staff members have their own document sets?**
A: Each browser session is independent. Each user can upload their own documents for their session.
